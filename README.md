This is my final project for AP CS A in java

This game is a text-based economic simulation rpg set in the 15th century.
You play as a travelling merchant in the middle ages with the goal of navigating a trade route from Venice to Constantinople.
The game uses a simple supply and demand system where the player interacts with guild factors at trading stands to trade up to 100 different types of historical goods. 
The aim is to buy low and sell high. 
Along the way random events might happen such as a bandit stealing their silver, or the player might hear a rumor that tells them what items might sell for higher in the towns down the road. 
When the player reaches the destination they will receive a final merchant rank based on their total wealth and survival status.
